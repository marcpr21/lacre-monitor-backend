<product_requirements>
The Lacre Monitor app is a photo monitoring tool for employees.
**Core Functionality:**
1.  **Employee Login:** Individual username/password login.
2.  **Photo Capture:** In-app camera only.
    *   **Seal Photos (Lacres):** Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays, 06:00-12:00.
    *   **Meter Photos (Medidor):** Daily, twice: 06:00-09:00 and 17:00-18:00.
3.  **Temporary Photos:** Not saved on phone.
4.  **Push Notifications:** Reminders for missed photos (1-hour for seals, 15-min for meters).
5.  **Admin Panel:** View all photos (employee, location, timestamp).
6.  **User Experience:** Simple, intuitive, cross-platform (Android & iOS).
7.  **Photo Storage:** Most recent photo only policy per type/period; teste user bypasses all restrictions.
8.  **Admin Enhancements:**
    *   **User Management:** Add, edit, delete, reset passwords for users.
    *   **Photo Organization:** Hierarchical, collapsible view: Date > Employee > Photo Type.
    *   **Compliance Report (Relatório de Conformidade):** Tab in admin panel to show missed photo submissions by employees, including dates/periods.
9.  **Deployment:** Keep app (especially backend) always online, generate Android APK.
10. **UI Fixes:** Adjust X (close) button position on iPhone photo modal, enable photo zoom.
</product_requirements>
