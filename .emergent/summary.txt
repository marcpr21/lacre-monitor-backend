<product_requirements>
The application, Lacre Monitor, is a photo monitoring tool for employees.
**Core Functionality:**
1.  **Employee Login:** Individual username/password login.
2.  **Photo Capture:** Employees must take photos directly via the in-app camera (no gallery uploads).
    *   **Seal Photos (Lacres):** Taken on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, specifically between 06:00 and 12:00.
    *   **Meter Photos (Medidor):** Taken daily, twice: once between 06:00-09:00 and again between 17:00-18:00.
3.  **Temporary Photos:** Photos are not saved on the employee's phone after submission.
4.  **Push Notifications:** Alerts are sent if photos are not taken within defined schedules (1-hour reminders for seals, 15-minute reminders for meters).
5.  **Admin Panel:** A separate web interface for administrators to view all photos, including employee, location, and timestamp data.
6.  **User Experience:** Simple, intuitive interface, cross-platform (Android & iOS).
7.  **Photo Storage:** Initially 15-day retention, now replaced by a most recent photo only policy per type/period. A special teste user bypasses all time and photo deletion restrictions.
8.  **Admin Enhancements:**
    *   **User Management:** Ability to add, edit, delete, and reset passwords for users.
    *   **Photo Organization:** Admin panel photos are grouped hierarchically by Date > Employee > Photo Type (Lacres/Medidor), with all levels being collapsible.
</product_requirements>
